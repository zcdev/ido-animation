iDo Project - Example: Space Odyssey

In this project, there are mainly 4 key steps / concepts:

  1. Use the template designed by the instructor.
  2. Choose a background image found by the instructor and use it as your theme: Space, Ocean, Farm, Town, Halloween.
  3. Change the characters in the template. Minimally 2 characters: Non-animated and animated. Non-animated character can be any one you found on the web. Animated character must be created by you. You can have up to 6 characters in total as maximum.
  4. Tweak the codes and customize to your needs. (We will cover the basic HTML 5 and CSS 3 features that are used in the template).
